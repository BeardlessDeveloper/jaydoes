What is Curb Kickin'?

You've got enough to deal with. Remembering which day the trash runs, making sure the cans are out on time, and hauling them back before the neighbors complain — that's one more thing on your list.

Jesse handles it. On your trash day, he moves your cans to the curb. After pickup, he brings them back. That's it. Simple and reliable.

Whether you're busy, out of town, elderly, or just done dealing with it — Curb Kickin' has you covered.
